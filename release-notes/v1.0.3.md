v1.0.3 - June 1, 2024

# Buffs
- Buff duration now stacks when collecting more than 1 buff of same type
- Auto-fire now fires automatically
  
# Aliens
- Stickbug
  - New level 23 invader with 4 health
  - Drops 3 damage rapid bomb, which can pierce shields
- Spider

- Mantis

- Shrimp
  - Update sprite
